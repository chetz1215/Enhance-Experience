<b><#selectbg_g>Android version</#></b> 
- Select your android version. 

<b><#selectbg_g>Select install or uninstall </#></b>
If you choose "Uninstall"
- "Optional Delete"- Delete any your prefer apps you installed through this mod.
- "Complete Uninstall" - Removes every file installed by the mod.

If you choose "Install"
then
<b><#selectbg_g>Select Clean flash or Dirty flash </#></b>
- Clean flash: You can delete stock los apps and install new apps. 
- Dirty flash: You can update your exisiting apps.

- Misc files are those files which installs other missing components which are needed to work with other apps.

- For eg: If you installing google phone make sure that you also tick "Google phone framework" , "Make google phone default phone app".
